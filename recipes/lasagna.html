Lasagna

Lasagna is an italian dish of needles, meat, and tomato sauce. It is delicious.

Ingredients

  • meat
  • garlic
  • noodles
  • tomatoes

Steps

  1. cook meat for 20 minutes
  2. cook tomatoes and garlic
  3. layer meat and noodles with the tomatoes and garlic
  4. cook for 30 minutes on 425 degrees farenheight
